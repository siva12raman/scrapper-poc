The space
This luxurious property features floor to ceiling Marvin windows providing stunning lake views. Brand new, professionally decorated home is ideal for entertaining, long term stays or simply enjoying a romantic stay.

The all-white modern interior boasts high-end appliances and fixtures making your stay a true luxury escape. Master bed with high end ‘TheCompanyStore’ bedding to give you the best night sleep. Two Custom XL bunk beds each boasting its own TV. The laundry room holds washer/dryer, built-in dresser storage. White sofas and indoor fireplace add to the beauty of the Villa.

Gourmet kitchen with 6 burner Zline gas stove, convection oven, built in fridge/freezer drawers and an Insta Hot water faucet for tea lovers. Nesspresso and vertuo cups are also provided! The kitchen is stocked with everything you need and more!

The Spa bathroom shines with white tiles, floating custom vanity, smart auto flush toilet, gorgeous large walk in shower and gilded mirror.

This amazing pet-friendly and kid-friendly property is located on a hill with its own private driveway. Private, well-lit 30 stairs lead you to the spectacular new Lake Villa. Being on a hill does have the 30 stair steps heading up to the house. The property is located in front of a main two lane road, and crossing the road is mandatory to access the private beach. Relax on the beautiful front deck with built-in hot tub and outdoor propane fireplace overlooking the glorious lake and mountain scenery.

Guest access
Our property is called Mountain Manor and all of our guests have access our private beach. The property is located in front of a main two lane road, and crossing the road is mandatory to access the private beach with 3 separate areas on the beach with fire pits to accommodate all of our guests
