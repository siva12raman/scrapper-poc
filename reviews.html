New laundry machines were a nice plus! It looked like there was also a crib to setup if desired.

The master bedroom has plenty of room. The second bedroom is tiny - bed is up against 2 walls and there is about 2.5 feet of space to walk along the one side of the bed and the foot of the bed. The tight space didn't bother us, but we had to keep our things in the adjacent laundry room (adding a dresser in the laundry room would be great).

The master bedroom windows open to the neighbors yard and the second bedroom windows open to the neighbors deck. When we visited, they were doing construction next door during the day, so when we were home, we kept the windows closed due to the noise. With the windows closed, the power tools didn't bother us. Since no one was staying next door, it was quiet at night, but might be a concern if you like to sleep with windows open or are a light sleeper.

We had an unfortunate experience that I doubt would happen to anyone else, but I mention it for 2 reasons: (1) to illustrate the host's communications (she did a nice job) and (2) as a precaution about the closeness of the neighbor unit. On our last night, we had to evacuate due to fumes coming from the next door unit (guessing they varnished the floors). Guita returned my calls/emails within an hour and tried to find us other accommodations. Due to the holiday, the only places with rooms wouldn't take our dog, so Guita kindly offered to take our dog for the night.
