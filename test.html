The space

Hot tub and outdoor fire pit are year-round amenities.

Neighborhood highlights

Our home is conveniently located near several local attractions that will enhance your stay in Owego. The Golf Club at Owego is adjacent to our property and offers stunning views of the Susquehanna River Valley, a full-service restaurant and bar, as well as elegant banquet and wedding facilities. For wine lovers, there are several local wineries within a short drive, including the award-winning Pheasant Ridge Vineyards and the Chateau Renaissance Wine Cellars. The area also offers a plethora of quaint coffee shops and popular breweries and cider mills.

If you're looking to enjoy the great outdoors, there are several parks nearby, including Hickories Park, which offers hiking trails, picnic areas, and a playground. A short drive away, you'll find the beautiful Watkins Glen State Park, Buttermilk Falls State Park, and Montour Falls, all offering stunning waterfalls, hiking trails, and scenic views.

For those interested in higher education, Ithaca College is just a short drive away and is home to several cultural attractions, including the Ithaca College Museum of Art and the Whalen Center for Music. And if you're feeling lucky, Tioga Downs Casino is also nearby and offers gaming, entertainment, and dining options.

Finally, no trip to the area would be complete without visiting the Seneca and Cayuga Finger Lakes, offering a wealth of outdoor activities, including fishing, boating, and kayaking. Our home provides easy access to all these attractions, making it the perfect choice for your next vacation. Book now and experience the best of Owego!
